Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of passionate tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into a global platform for innovation and collaboration.

Our Mission

TechCon’s mission is to inspire innovation, support diversity in tech, and foster the global exchange of ideas that shape the future of technology.

Past Speakers

Over the years, we’ve welcomed many tech visionaries to our stage:

Dr. Ada Smith

AI expert and pioneer in machine learning ethics. Dr. Smith’s 2020 keynote on “Ethical AI” reshaped how the industry views responsibility in tech.

James Okoro

CTO of GlobalTech and advocate for sustainable computing. His 2022 talk emphasized the environmental impact of data centers and green tech practices.
